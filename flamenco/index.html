Flamenco

Flamenco is a style of folk music popular in many parts of the world, but more dominantly so in Spain. It is a mysterious style of music given that the people who popularzed it - the Nomadic people of Romani, often called Gypsies, or Gitanos - do not have written records. There are traces of influences from Africa and India. It is played on an acoustic guitar and often accompanied by palmas, baile, or cante.
