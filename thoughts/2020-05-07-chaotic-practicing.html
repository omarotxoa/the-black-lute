Chaotic practicing

May 07, 2020

So I've been playing a bit every day. Anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour or more.

I've mostly been practicing the Study in E Minor by Tarrega and Villa-Lobos' Prelude No.4. I tend to just play those pieces over and over as its more fun than practicing from the classical guitar method ebook I mentioned in my previous post for example.

I do acknowledge that it is important to have focused practice of technique and such, but its such a daunting task for me at the moment that I'm just happy with consistently just picking up the guitar and playing.

I plan to figure out a more rigorous and structured practice schedule later on, but for now I'm quite happy with just actually having got started.
