About

The purpose of this page is to inform those who are curious. There are brief outlines of the content you will find on the webste, as well as other information one might be interested in.

General Introduction

The Black Lute is a website with the following purposes:

  • As an effort to provide written content in a world full of Video.
  • As a place to explore and share the darker, sometimes dissonant (yet beautiful) shades of music: Flamenco, Mexican Folk, Darker Blues, etc.

Technical Details

The Black Lute is a static website, coded in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. It is hosted on Digitial Ocean.

Font families include Roboto Slab for titles and Yaldevi for any other text.
